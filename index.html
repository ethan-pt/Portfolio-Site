About Me

I started coding in early 2021 after I had an idea for my first IOT project, assuming I’d need some programming skills to get it started. As it turns out, I was right, although I soon realized that my project would be better implemented as a smartphone app that likely already exists, but, this project ended up being just the push I needed to realize just how much I enjoyed programming. Believe it or not, I really enjoy being entirely lost and directionless after diving head-first into a project or feature. It allows me to take the time to gain new skills or utilize old ones in ways I hadn’t considered previously, which is exciting to me.

Outside of programming, I’m also a hobbyist photographer (I took all images featured on this website), a huge car guy, and a renowned cat dad. My favorite parts of all of these things are their many intricacies, encouraging me to dive deeper into new concepts, and increasing my effectiveness and proficiency across the board.
